THE PROBLEM S(ECURE) AID IS TRYING TO SOLVE

SAID aims to eliminate inefficiency and corruption in sending relief donations to those facing humanitarian crises

  • Current System

    Donors send money to NGO's whom disperse donations among governments. Governments then use donations to buy relief supplies and aid for those who need it.

  • Corruption

    Corruption with the allocation of funds is a pressing issue. For example in 2011, over $300 million of Somalian government funds and another $70 million from Arab donors were missing from its treasury.

  • Process

    Donors send money to NGO where it is converted into a cryptocurrency. This cryptocurrency is sent to affected where it can only be exchanged for certain supplies.

  • Advantages

    This process ensures transperancy, flexibility and security. Integrated cryptocurrency transactions are instantaneous to ensure urgency during extreme crises.
