Blood:Water

Blood:Water focuses on partnering with Africa's hidden heros to provide support them in the communities they serve, in regards to both HIV and water.

  • Specific Issue (Health and Water):

    •Providing clean water supplies.

    •Solving improper sanitation.

    •Solving insufficient hygiene materials.

    •Preventing AIDS/HIV.




  • Affected Region:

    Africa: Algeria, Angola, Benin, Botswana, Burkina Faso, Burundi...

  • Solution:

    •Finance and implement proper toilet systems;

    •Finance and implement clean water systems;

    •Finance the implementation proper hygiene equipment;

    •Support infrastructure for AIDS treatment.
