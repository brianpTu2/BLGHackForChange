Water Aid

Wateraid focuses on helping people break free from poverty and change their lives for good through the three essentials of clean water, decent toilets and good hygiene.

  • Specific Issue (Water):

    •1 in 9 people don’t have clean water close to home.

    •1 in 3 people don’t have a working toilet of their own.

  • Affected Region:

    Global: Malawi, United States, Bangladesh, United Kingdom, India, Japan, Sweden, Canada, Australia

  • Solution:

    •Finance and distribute decent hygiene products;

    •Finance the implementation of toilets;

    •Finance and create clean water infrastructure.
