Action Against Hunger

Action Against Hunger is a global humanitarian organization that targets both the causes and effects of hunger. Their primary target areas are Nutrition/Health, Water/Sanitation, and Food Security/Livelihoods.

  • Specific Issue (Food/Water/Health):

    •Solving undernutrition in third world countries

    •Providing safe drinking water.

    •Providing improved sanitation

  • Affected Region:

    Worldwide

  • Solution:

    Provide Access to Safe Water, Promote Sanitation/Hygiene, Ensure Lasting Change. Gather, Analyze, and Share Data on Undernutrition, Treat Acute Undernutrition, Prevent Undernutrition, Strengthen Local Capacity and Build Resilience
