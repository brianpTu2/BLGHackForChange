European Civil Protections And Humanitarian Aid Operations

European Civil Protection and Humanitarian Aid Operations (ECHO) provides relief in all major crisis zones around the world including Syria, South Sudan, Yemen, and Ukraine. It also contributes to tackling the refugee crisis in Europe, also by mobilizing EU civil protection channels.

  • Specific Issue (Disaster Relief):

    • ECHO has monitored 253 disasters and has received over 150 requests for assistance.

    • In 2013, the EU spent €56.2 bn on development aid.

  • Affected Region:

    Global: Most African, Middle-Eastern, and Central American Countries

  • Solution:

    •Constantly Strengthens Disaster Prevention and Preparedness;

    •Provide Faster and More Efficient Disaster Response;

    •Enhance International Cooperation
